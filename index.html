GLOBAL STATE OF ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENCE

The Global AI Index (GAII) reflects a nation's current state on Artificial Intelligence. Launched in 2019 by Tortoise, the Global Artificial Intelligence Index is a comprehensive metric that quantifies the AI landscape across a robust set of factors, and remains as one of the leaders in understanding the progression of Artificial Intelligence.

At its most recent release, the Global AI Index includes 62 countries. Each country is given an overall score, the Artificial Ingtelligence Index, which is based on 3 key pillars: Implementation, Innovation and Investment.

IMPLEMENTATION

Implementation is representative of a country's practical stance on Artificial Intelligence. It is indicative of the human and physical resources available in a country to develop AI Solutions.


The talent attribute reflects the density of specialists in Artificial Intelligence within a nation, mainly calculated by considering the number of AI and data professionals in the country and AI-related GitHub and Stack Overflow activity from users from that particular country.

The infrastructure attribute reflects a country's ability to provide the resources required (electricity, internet access, databases, computational specifications etc.) to develop and promote a sustainable AI ecosystem.

Operating Environment relates to the pubic perception of a country's stance on Artificial Intelligence, and factors gender diversity, transparency, and cybersecurity into consideration.

INNOVATION

Innovation relates to a country's capacity for Artificial Intelligence in the future, and measures the extent to which a country is pushing the boundaries in what is currently possible in AI.

The research attribute reflects a countries role within the AI research community, notably the research from a country that has been published, and how much these works have been cited.

Development reflects the the actual new techniques, methods and technologies in AI being developed by a country. This is measured by factors like the number of commits on pilular open-source AI packages.

INVESTMENT

Investment relates to a country's financial committment to Artificial Intelligence, as measured by government strategies and commercial ventures.

The commercial attributes reflects the AI-related commercial ventures taken by a country. Its calculation considers the funding for AI companies and startups, and the number of AI companies and startups.

The government stratey attribute encompasses a country's past and current AI strategies.
